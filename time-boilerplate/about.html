Time is omnipresent. It infiltrates every facet of life.
You can be on time, not on time, early or late.
These attachments with time factor into every experience of our lives.
Being out of time can leave one feeling disappointed and with a need to catch up.
The clock signifies how we become attuned to being in the moment.
If our visual and auditory senses process information that is not aligned,
it becomes out of time, or out of sync.
